I'm an educator who taught mathematics to underprivileged students for over a decade, founded a Delhi non-profit in 2012, and taught myself full-stack engineering to turn that mission into free, ad-free learning platforms used by students around the world. Today I design, code and ship an entire EdTech ecosystem — **solo** — using AI tools and agents to move at the speed of a team.

Everything below is real, in active use, and made for learners first.

### 🚀 What I'm building

| Project | What it is | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **[Vidaara](https://vidaara.org)** | Free e-learning portal — Class 1–12 study material (Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Science, Computer Science, English, Economics, Accountancy) plus exam prep for **SAT, CAT, SSC CGL, CTET, JEE, NEET &amp; IMO**. Thousands of MCQs, full mock tests, interactive labs, an AI study tutor and a Q&amp;A forum. | 🟢 Live |
| **[Ganak](https://ganak.vidaara.org)** | A mathematics engine that solves K-12, SAT, JEE, CAT, GRE &amp; Olympiad problems **step by step** — a rule-based symbolic solver, not a black box. Powers the maths across Vidaara. | 🟢 Live |
| **[Ankora](https://ankora.vidaara.org)** | A math RPG for kids (Grades 3–8) where **maths is the weapon** — get a sum right, cast the spell. Playable on the web; Android on the way. | 🟢 Live (web) |
| **Words of Vidaara (WOV)** | A fast, fun vocabulary game built in Flutter — learning English the playful way. | 📱 Coming to Google Play |
| **Vidaara App** | The Flutter companion app that brings the full Vidaara portal to Android. | 📱 In development |

### 🌱 Why

Good education shouldn't depend on what your family can pay. Vidaara is my attempt to put genuinely good study material, practice and tools in front of any student with a phone — for free.

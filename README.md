## 👋 Hi, I'm Sachin Sharma — `@sachinrajsharma`

**Mathematics Educator · Social Entrepreneur · Self-taught Developer**
Founder, [Udgam Welfare Foundation](https://www.udgamwelfarefoundation.com) (since 2012)

I build free, technology-driven education platforms that remove the barriers of cost and geography — so that talent, not a family's bank balance, decides a child's future.

---

### 🎯 What drives me

I know first-hand what it costs to be denied an education. That conviction became a mission: **education is the greatest equalizer.** For over a decade I've taught mathematics to underprivileged students — many of whom lived and studied in my own home — and watched them go from families earning under $100/month to engineering careers. Today I scale that work through code.

For me, real education does more than build intellect — it builds integrity, empathy, and the will to lift others.
